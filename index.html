FAQ

Any Questions? Answered

Get answers to common questions about our mobile detailing services, booking process, and what to expect.

We provide mobile detailing services throughout the Greater Vancouver area, including Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Surrey, and surrounding areas. As a mobile service, we come directly to your location, whether that's your home, office, or another convenient spot.
A full detail typically takes 3-5 hours depending on your vehicle's size and condition. This includes thorough interior cleaning, exterior wash, paint correction, and protective coating application. We'll provide a more specific time estimate when you book based on your chosen service package.
We bring all necessary equipment and supplies, including water and power sources. We just need access to your vehicle and enough space around it to work safely. If possible, parking in a shaded area is helpful, but not required. We'll handle everything else!
We use only premium, professional-grade detailing products that are safe for your vehicle and the environment. Our selection includes top brands known for their quality and effectiveness. All products are carefully chosen to protect your vehicle's surfaces while delivering outstanding results.
For optimal maintenance, we recommend a full detail every 3-4 months. However, this can vary based on your driving conditions, climate, and how you use your vehicle. Regular maintenance details can help protect your car's finish and maintain its value over time.
Yes! We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with any aspect of our service, we'll make it right. We also offer specific guarantees on our paint protection and ceramic coating services.
